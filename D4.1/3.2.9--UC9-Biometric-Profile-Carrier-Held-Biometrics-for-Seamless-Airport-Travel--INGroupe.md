### UC 9: Biometric Profile - Carrier Held Biometrics - for Seamless Airport Travel (IN Groupe)
This use case brings together IN Groupe, lead to this UC, as the issuance biometric profil attestation issuer, France Titres — Agence Nationale des Titres Sécurisés (ANTS) as the EUDI Wallet provider and Marseille Airport as remote and proximity Relying Party for the verification of the biometric profil. 
To work jointly on this use case, we organized regular meetings and collaborated on scope, workflows and documentation. 
Our goal is to find improve our current biometric profil solution by integrating the Wallet infrastructure, providing users a better user experience, more security, and with the full control of their identity.
